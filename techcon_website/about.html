Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small gathering of passionate technologists. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees and industry leaders. Each year, TechCon has introduced new themes, technologies, and opportunities for collaboration, making it a cornerstone of the tech community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster learning, and connect people who are passionate about technology. We believe in empowering individuals and organizations to shape the future through knowledge sharing, hands-on experiences, and meaningful connections.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace
AI Pioneer

Renowned for her groundbreaking work in artificial intelligence and computer science education, Dr. Lovelace inspired a new generation of innovators at TechCon 2019.

Linus Torvalds
Creator of Linux

As the creator of the Linux kernel, Linus shared insights on open-source collaboration and the future of software at TechCon 2021.

Grace Hopper
Programming Language Visionary

Grace Hopper's keynote at TechCon 2022 highlighted the importance of accessible programming and diversity in tech.
